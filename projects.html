MIND-JOURNEY MENTAL HEALTH

AI-powered mental health risk assessment platform built with React, Flask, and scikit-learn. Complete machine learning pipeline includes data preprocessing with pandas, feature engineering, model training with 85%+ accuracy target, and real-time prediction inference.
